Exploration is a method commonly used by designers to develop a critical thinking on open or complex subjects.

To make your exploration, follow carefully these steps:

  1. Choose carefully relevant keywords for your subject. Then make a research using these keywords on the Learning Hub website or Google
  2. Find an interesting resource and explore it! Explore a resource mean just take spontaneous notes reading or, watching or listening a resource as if you are thinking out loud. Your brain is designed to make connections spontaneously. Feel free. No matter what thought this resource gives you, note it.
  3. Document this node (one resource & one spontaneous thought) on your Mindmeister mindmap.
  4. Choose new relevant keywords from your precedent step as it was a new subject.
  5. Repeat the four previous steps at least 10 times.
